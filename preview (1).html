District Internet Safety & Phishing Awareness Training

This professional training module helps staff identify phishing emails, suspicious internet behavior, and understand how online actions create a digital footprint.

Participants will move through realistic scenarios designed to strengthen judgment, increase awareness of online risks, and build confidence in recognizing misleading information, suspicious requests, and digital habits that can affect both personal security and professional reputation.
